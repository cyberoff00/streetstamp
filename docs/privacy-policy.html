Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Worldo ("we", "our", or "the App") is a journey tracking and city discovery application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: Email address, display name, and authentication credentials when you create an account.
  • Journey data: Routes, locations, timestamps, and photos you capture during journeys.
  • Social data: Friend connections, postcards, and messages you send or receive through the App.

Information collected automatically

  • Precise location: GPS coordinates collected during journey tracking (only when you actively start tracking or enable background tracking).
  • Motion and fitness data: Step count and motion activity (walking, running, driving) used to enhance journey recording accuracy.
  • Health data: Daily step counts read from HealthKit, displayed in your Lifelog. Workout session data written to maintain stable background tracking.
  • Device information: App Store region, device locale, and app version for feature availability and localization.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Record and display your journeys and city visits
  • Show your daily step count and movement history in Lifelog
  • Enable social features (friend connections, postcards) where available
  • Sync your data across devices via iCloud
  • Improve journey tracking accuracy using motion data
  • Determine feature availability based on your region

3. Data Storage and Security

Your journey data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via iCloud (Apple CloudKit). Account and social data is stored on our secure servers. We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission and JWT-based authentication to protect your account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data may be shared only in these limited cases:

  • With friends you connect with: Your display name, avatar, city cards, and postcards are visible to friends you add.
  • Map services: Your location is sent to Apple Maps for reverse geocoding (converting coordinates to city names) and optional route snapping.
  • As required by law: We may disclose information if required by applicable law or legal process.

5. Your Choices

  • You can disable location tracking at any time through iOS Settings.
  • You can deny Health/Motion data access without affecting core app functionality.
  • You can delete your journey data within the App.
  • You can request account deletion by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

Worldo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by updating the "Last updated" date above.
